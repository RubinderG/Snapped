Welcome to Snapped!

Snapped is an online photo book of photographs snapped by me, Rubinder.

During the Global Pandemic, I had a lot more spare time due to government restrictions limiting my activities so to fill the time I worked my way through my
'Always wanted to do but never had the spare time' check-list and one of the items was to learn more about photography. The online course was great and I've snapped some awesome pictures of my life through the lenses of Android and Apple mobile phones.

Through attending SheCodes I've learned the skills to be able to host my pictures on their own website and that's how Snapped was created!

If there's a moment you don't want to forget, get your phone out and get it Snapped and every time you see that Snap you'll be transported to that moment in that very instance.

To me that's the power of photography, making moments everlasting...
